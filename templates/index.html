About This Calculator

This tool helps you estimate your carbon footprint based on your consumption habits. Our goal is to provide a simple way for individuals to understand their environmental impact and take steps towards a more sustainable lifestyle.

What is a Carbon Footprint?

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases (including carbon dioxide and methane) that are generated by our actions. It's a measure of our impact on the environment and is typically measured in kilograms or tons of CO2 equivalent (kgCO2e) per year.

By understanding our carbon footprint, we can make more informed decisions to reduce it, such as:

  • Conserving energy at home
  • Using sustainable transportation
  • Reducing flight travel
  • Supporting reforestation efforts

How We Calculate

Our calculator uses the following emission factors:

  • Electricity: 0.5 kgCO2e per €
  • Natural Gas: 0.2 kgCO2e per €
  • Car Travel: 0.12 kgCO2e per km
  • Flights: 250 kgCO2e per flight (average)
